Notes: FCC regulation (47 CFR § 64.2011) became effective in 2007. If the FCC penalty reflects regulatory burden,
the coefficient should be non-significant pre-2007 and significant post-2007. If the penalty reflects industry
characteristics, the coefficient should be similar across both periods.

Key Finding: FCC effect emerges after regulation (Model 3 > Model 2), supporting interpretation that
the penalty comes from regulatory constraints, not pre-existing industry characteristics.
